Outputs The Student Chronicles (Miegunyah Press, 2006). When the book came out I did a lot of interviews (radio and print) and some writers' festival appearances so there are likely to be other records in media archives etc.
Other information I am still in contact with nearly all the people interviewed (most of them were my close friends at the time I interviewed them) and I plan to contact them about completing a retrospective consent form that would ask for permission for other/future uses / archiving of the material. I started digitising the tapes myself at home but they are not fantastic quality, partly because the cassette playback machine I was using was not the one I originally recorded on and the speed is slightly different. I have adjusted the speed in my audio editing software but all in all, this is not archival quality digitisation! It's me figuring out how to do it so that I have a backup.
