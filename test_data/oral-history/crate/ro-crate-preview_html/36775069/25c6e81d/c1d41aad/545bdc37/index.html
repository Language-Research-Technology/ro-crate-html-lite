Description Maximum of 5 hours of interviews in 1979 by prominent local printer, activist and historian, Les Barnes (1905-1994). Interview was undertaken by field work student Norma Martin. Start of each tape includes short list of topics covered. Currently analogue only but with aim to digitize in 2025.

Other information Les Barnes wrote a number of articles and books that covered similar topics. He also contributed to many taped presentations on local history topics that are in the Talks on Brunswick/Talks on Moreland collection. A 1982 oral history interview with Alistair Thomson is at the Australian War Memorial about the Brunswick RSL. An archive of his papers is at the Australian National University.
