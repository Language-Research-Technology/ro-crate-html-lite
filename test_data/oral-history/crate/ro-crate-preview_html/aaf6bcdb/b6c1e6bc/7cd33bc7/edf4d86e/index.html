Description The history of migrant settlement, focusing on several waves of arrivals, in the Springvale region. The nationality of migrants interviewed include British, southern European, and south-east Asian (including refugees from Vietnam and Cambodia). There are more than 200 detailed interviews conducted between 1990-1993, and a second series of interviews, totalling nearly 30 conducted in 2023. All of the second series of interviews have been professionally transcribed, also close to 100 of the first series.

Collection characteristics

Purpose The first series of interviews were collected with funding from the Australian Research Council and were utilised in the publication 'Fourteen Lives'. The objective was to record the experiences of different immigrant waves, including their lived experiences in an ethnically diverse community. The second series was funded by the Scanlon Foundation and its objective was to record experiences in an ethnically diverse community during the COVID pandemic.
