Description As part of the 2017 Melbourne Fringe Festival, Memento Media and Moreland City Council presented ReCollection at the Coburg Carnivale. ReCollection celebrated, shared and captured memories of Moreland's places, history and faces. The installation showcased right oral history based documentaries, based on six oral history interviews. Also, ten Merri-bek locals shared stories and photos in the ReCollection Recorder during the exhibition.
