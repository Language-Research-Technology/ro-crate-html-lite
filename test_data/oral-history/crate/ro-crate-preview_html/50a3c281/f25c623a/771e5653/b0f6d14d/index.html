Description The collection comprises some 27 oral history interviews with former and active members or associates of the Italian Federation of Emigrant Workers and their Families, chiefly of the Melbourne branch. The interviews were recorded between May 2002 and May 2004. They took place in Australia (13 in Victoria and 5 in South Australia), Italy (6 in Rome and 2 in Modena) and United States (1 in New York).

Collection characteristics

Purpose The oral history interviews were collected as part of a PhD project held at La Trobe University and titled "History and Collective Memory of the Italian Migrant Workers' Organisation FILEF in 1970s Melbourne".
