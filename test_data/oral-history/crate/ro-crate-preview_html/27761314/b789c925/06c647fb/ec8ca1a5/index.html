Description Cassette recordings of Pioneer Dinners from 1963 to 2015, some personal interviews either cassette or CD and some now on Victorian Collections but not for sharing , CDs of photos, DVDs of various local topics. Film of historical places 1960's to 1980's. Some of these have been transferred to digital format (USB). Magnetic tapes of some speeches of local interest.

Outputs Some small booklets have been produced from local people's childhood stories of living in Port Fairy. These were generated from an exhibition surrounding a new book launch of "A Port Fairy Childhood" written in the 1800's by Margaret Emily Brown.
Other information We have been working to try and turn our collection of cassette tapes, magnetic tapes, film of various sizes from as far back as the 1990's when CD's were introduced. Our photos were first scanned to CD by a dedicated volunteer but we still receive an amazing number of photographs either digital or actual and struggle to keep our data bases etc up to date. We are still struggling to keep up with the new technology.
