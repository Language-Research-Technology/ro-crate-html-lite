Description This oral history collection is a key outcome of Associate Professor Nathalie Huynh Chau Nguyen's project on "Vietnamese in the Australian Defence Force" (2017-18) that was funded by a Monash Faculty of Arts Seed Scheme. It contains oral history interviews with male and female current and former service personnel of the Australian Army, the Royal Australian Air Force and the Royal Australian Navy.

Collection characteristics

Purpose This oral history collection was the result of an innovative scoping project on Vietnamese who are current and former members of the Australian Defence Force (ADF). Vietnamese in the ADF are a non-traditional and under- represented group. This project focused on their life history, their family history and their reasons for enlisting as well as their ADF experiences.
