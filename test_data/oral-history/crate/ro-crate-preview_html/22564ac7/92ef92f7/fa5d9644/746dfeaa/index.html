Description A collection of oral history interviews with 24 refugees, asylum seekers, and service providers who have worked with refugees and asylum seekers with a connection to Ballarat, Victoria. All have been residents of Ballarat at some point from 1994 to the current day. Some refugees came through government programs and pilots, others coming by boat.
