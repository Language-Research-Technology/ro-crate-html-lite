Description Interviews with Australians/people who lived in Australia in the 1990s and 2000s about their experiences with, understandings of, and relationships to the internet, computers, and computer networking. The main focus is on the period from 1989 to 2010, however, interviewees spoke about experiences from the 1960s/70s until present (the 1980s in particular are also discussed). They discuss the internet as a technology, a social space, a useful site of communication and information (among other things), as well as the place it found within their lives at various points in time.
