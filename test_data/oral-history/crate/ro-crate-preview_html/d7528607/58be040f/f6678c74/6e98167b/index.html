Description Various. The Memories of Malvern Oral History Project involved interviews with people who had lived or worked most of their lives in the Malvern area. Memories of the past for the future: oral recollections from Prahran, sought to document the experiences of working class people living in Prahran. The Malvern Oral History Project includes interviews with members of the Greek and Italian community who have settled in the Malvern area. Oral histories with Prahran residents speaking about their recollections of World War 2. Young Voices of the Pandemic interviewed ten young people about their experiences of COVID-19. Older Voices of the Pandemic interviewed older local residents about their experiences of COVID-19. By our Deeds: Reflections on terms in the Mayoral Office interviewed former mayors of the municipality of Stonnington, and the former municipalities of Prahran and Malvern. Talking Stories: narratives by women in Malvern interviewed local women about growing up or living in the Malvern area. MECWA Centenary Life Stories Project aimed to acknowledge the community spirit, particularly the voluntary work, of senior members of the local community.
