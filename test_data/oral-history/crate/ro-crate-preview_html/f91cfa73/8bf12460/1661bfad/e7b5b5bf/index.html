Description A collection of interviews conducted by Jim Mitchell in the 1990s to facilitate his writing of a battalion history of the Australian 2/2nd Pioneer Battalion. The soldiers enlisted in 1940-41, trained in Australia and Palestine (1940-41), fought the Vichy French in Syria (1941), fought the Japanese on Java (1942), became prisoners of war and a labouring force of the Japanese in South-East Asia (where they worked on the Burma-Thailand Railway) and in Japan (1942-45). Recordings vary from cassette tapes to DAT; some are also made into mp3 files preparatory to being transcribed. Transcriptions are typed versions of the interviews, but without time indications.
