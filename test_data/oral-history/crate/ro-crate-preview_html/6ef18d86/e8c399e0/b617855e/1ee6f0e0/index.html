Description The collection include early years at the Powlett Rivers Coalfields 1909 and Wonthaggi's initial development from tents to buildings. But then life in the decades including Mine Disasters, Strikes and Women's Groups. Migrants arriving to work in the coal mines Arts, social, family, sport, shops, farming, local towns, and much more capturing the life from the first town's mining roots to today.

Collection characteristics

Purpose Initial collection was tape recording from guest speakers at meetings. Then capturing stories from prominent residents, long term residents. To the current annual 15 Minutes of History daily talks each January videoed to be kept for generations.
