Description I am noting three collections: 1) my PhD interviews with teachers of the Holocaust in a selection of Jewish schools in Melbourne and New York; 2) a small project on Australian Jewish sexuality involving an interview with a Jewish lesbian couple who were wedded in a synagogue (before Marriage Equality was legalised) and the rabbi who conducted the wedding ceremony; 3) my postdoctoral interviews with people who have worked in Australian child refugee policy-making

Collection characteristics

Purpose 1) to undertake research for my PhD 2) to undertake research for a small, independent, project that I conducted when I wanted to continue original research but wasn't employed in a job which supported me to do research 3) to undertake research for my ARC-funded postdoc (which was part of an ARC Laureate project, led by Professor Joy Damousi)
