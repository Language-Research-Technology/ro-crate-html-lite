Date Recording period: 1970s, 1980s, 1990s
Description Our local studies room, while not exclusively focused on the collection of oral history, does contain within its scope audio recordings and transcripts of oral stories and their ilk. There is some collection of spoken word poetry, memoirs, interviews and even some ballads. The subject matter relates to East Gippsland, its history, locally significant and personally significant, and content relating to the area or significant persons from the region.

Collection characteristics

Purpose This part of the collection was contributed to fulfil the aims of the local studies room, which is to collect and create a history of the region and its peoples, to tell the story of the region to itself. Why there are recordings has not been fully specified.
