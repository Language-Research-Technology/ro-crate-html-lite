Description Ongoing collection of interview videos of people with significant connections to Melbourne Royal, particularly relating to the Melbourne Royal Show. This includes farmers and agriculturalists, exhibitors, staff and contractors, volunteer officials such as judges, councillors, commercial stallholders, and ride operators.

Collection characteristics

Purpose The Oral History program was created to identify, collect, record and document the experiences of significant individuals who have been involved in Melbourne Royal and Victorian agriculture in various ways. In particular it presented an opportunity to capture important information from a group of key individuals whose decades of knowledge, experience and understanding of both agriculture and Melbourne Royal would have been lost had action not been taken.

Other information The collection is saved across two hard drive for now, but given the size of the files, we've had some difficulty resourcing the production of adequate back ups that also have adequate ways to document their save location. We are also uncertain about whether we will be able to keep up with potential technological changes over the next fifty years. Accessibility has also been an issue, as recordings have not been adequately reviewed and edited to be suited to a public platform. We have been able to share them by request on an individual basis.
