Description This is a recording of the Andrew Ross Museum's AGM in 2009, held at Le Pine Funerals chapel in Eltham. Each year the museum has relied on the generosity of local organisations to provide a venue for its AGM, as the museum itself is too small for that purpose. It is relatively unusual for the AGMs to be recorded. In this instance Simon Braid of Le Pine Eltham offered the use of the recording facility already setup at the venue. The video is a snapshot of the business of the Andrew Ross Museum at a particular moment in history. The meeting was hosted by outgoing President John Austin and incoming president Warwick Leeson, with Guest Speaker Mick Woiwod (author of several local history books).
