Description This collection contains oral histories with current and retired staff, volunteers and community members with involvement in the North Richmond Community Health Centre. It was created in order to mark the 50th Anniversary of the Centre.

Collection characteristics

Purpose The collection was created to mark the 50th Anniversary of the Centre, with the development of some digital (video) history pieces as well as to inform a written history of the Centre. They also provide historical insights into the community of people living in the high-rise public housing towers of North Richmond.
