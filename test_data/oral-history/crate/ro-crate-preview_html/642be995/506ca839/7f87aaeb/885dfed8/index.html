Description In 2027 the Royal Australasian College of Surgeons will commemorate its centenary and the oral history project is part of this celebration.

Collection characteristics

Purpose The aim of the project is to capture personal stories of Fellows and staff of the College in order to build a collection of unique and valuable oral histories. This would be an outstanding way to gather information about key eras, events, individuals, achievements and personalities in the College's past that can be drawn on by upcoming and future history projects.
