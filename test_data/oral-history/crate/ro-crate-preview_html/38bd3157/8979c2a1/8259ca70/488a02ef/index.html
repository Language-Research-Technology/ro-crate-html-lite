Description The collection comprises various interviews with key representatives of the Australian Jewish community regarding the history of the community dating back to the First Fleet with convicts arriving from England. It also records history of Australian Jews through the different waves of migration after the first fleet, particularly after 1945 as a result of the WWII and the Holocaust.

Collection characteristics

Purpose Purpose of the collection is to harness the personal stories and recollections of Australian Jews to fully record the history and experiences of this evolving community.
