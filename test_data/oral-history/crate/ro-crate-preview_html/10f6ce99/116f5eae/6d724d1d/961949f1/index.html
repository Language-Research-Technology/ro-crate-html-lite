Description Pentridge Voices records personal accounts of people who were directly involved with Pentridge Prison in Coburg, Victoria, in order to preserve this firsthand history. The collection includes interviews with former prisoners, former staff, and individuals with a unique connection to the former prison.

Collection characteristics

Purpose Pentridge Voices' collection was created to preserve the personal histories of those connected to Pentridge Prison. The purpose is to document the human side of the notorious institution by gathering firsthand oral histories from former prisoners, staff, and others, ensuring their unique experiences are not forgotten and lost.
