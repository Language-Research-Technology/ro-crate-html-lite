Description This collection contains oral history interviews with founding members, volunteers, staff and musicians to document the history of the organisation at the point of celebrating 40 years of operations.

Collection characteristics

Purpose The collection was created to mark the 40th anniversary of the organisation, but also to create a record of the impact of the organisation on the development of the music scene in Victoria and the important role of culturally diverse musicians in that music and cultural scene over time. It provides important insights into the cultural history of Victoria since the 1970s and the evolving role of multicultural arts in that context.
