Description Queer Histories, Queer Futures is an audio journey into the stories of our LGBTQIA+ communities in Merri-bek, past, present and future. From pubs to squats, protests to parties, discover Merri-bek’s queer history as told by the people who shaped it. Walk the streets with us over fourteen episodes to discover the collective histories of LGBTQIA+ organising, activism and community in Merri-bek and our imaginings for the future.
